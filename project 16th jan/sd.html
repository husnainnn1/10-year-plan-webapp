How to build strong relationships?

I know that building strong relationships with friends and family members takes time and effort. One of the most important things I'm trying to do is to make sure I am communicating effectively. This means I need to actively listening to the other person, being open and honest about my thoughts and feelings, and making an effort to understand their perspective. Additionally, it's important to make time for one another and to make sure that I am spending quality time together. This can include things like going on trips together, taking part in shared hobbies and interests, or simply spending time at home together. With a combination of effective communication, shared experiences, and genuine care, I am of the opinion that I can build and maintain strong relationships with friends and family members over the next 10 years.
